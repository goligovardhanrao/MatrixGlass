MATRIX GLASS

“FIRST GLASS TEMPERING FACTORY” in Capital city of ANDHRA PRADESH, VIJAYAWADA, started in the year 2015, in short span of time we served well desirable projects across the country.

With an experience of over 5 Decades in glass processing industry this factory is result of highly enthusiastic third generation involving into business, our most advanced manufacturing facility that’s spread out over 40,000 Sq.Ft. We are the manufacturers and exporters of highest quality Toughened / Tempered Glass, Laminated Glass, Insulated Glass units to meet the ever rising demands of glass industry.

Today we are occupying a prominent position in the market which has come our way after a period of intense hard work. We understand our customer's requirements first and then initiate our production process which greatly helps us in producing as per the specification given by them.

We are equipped with modern and latest imported machinery most of them PLC controlled to provide highest quality processed glass meeting national and global quality standards and are comprehensively equipped with a highly qualified, experienced, and versatile workforce, which uncompromisingly adheres to quality and product efficiency.

The glass we process are highly used in Architectural Glass, Automotive Glass, Railways, Furniture, Solar Panels etc.,

We have a production capacity of 800sqmtr per day with thickness from 4MM to 19MM and can process Clear / Tinted / and all types of Low E (soft / Hard) Coated glass.

Having wide network of distribution with self owned fleets is the success story of this company.
